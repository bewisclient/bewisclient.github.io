Bewisclient
Bewisclient is an innovative Minecraft fabric mod that enriches gameplay with features like Fullbright, Shulkerbox Tooltip, Custom Blockhit, on-screen widgets, improved visibility, Held Item Info, and more. Its user-friendly Mod Menu and support for multiple translations make it a great addition to any modpack. Credit to various sources for inspiration and code implementation. Elevate your Minecraft experience with Bewisclient!

Here are some of the features of Bewisclient:

- Fullbright:  This feature makes the game world completely bright, even in dark areas. This can be helpful for exploring caves or fighting mobs at night.
- Shulkerbox Tooltip:  This feature shows a tooltip when you hover over a Shulkerbox, displaying the contents of the box. This can be helpful for inventory management.
- On-screen widgets:  This feature allows you to display information on the screen, such as your biome, fps, and coordinates.
- Held Item Info:  This feature shows information about the item you are holding, such as its enchantments.

Bewisclient is a great mod for anyone who wants to enhance their Minecraft experience. It is easy to use and has a wide range of features that can make the game more enjoyable.
